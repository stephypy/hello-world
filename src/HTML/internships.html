Internships.


The word internship does not necessarily exist in my native language (spanish), so at first I was confused why people at my school kept mentioning it. I had no idea it was possible to temporarily work at a tech company as a student, so with the advice and encouragement of several upperclassmen, I started applying to internships every year. Who would have known? Now I can share with you what I learned in my internships. Enjoy!
